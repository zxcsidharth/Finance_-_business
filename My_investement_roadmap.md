
- Jab investement karna hai to isko aise karna hai ki ek taraf wealth grow kare aur dusri taraf **_wealth preserve_** ho.
- Wealth preserve karne ka matlab ye hai ki wealth jitna hai wo kam na hoye aur badhe thora kam growth k sath v to chalega. 
- And **_Wealth grow_** karne ka matlab hai ki money ko aisa jagah lagao ki thora return acha khasa mille bhale thora risky investement kyun na ho.


#### Wealth Preservation:-
isme ye sab fund ko add kar sakte hain like
- Index fund:- Like Nifty index, Nasdaq index, S&P index
- ETF's:- isme gold ETF's ho sakte hain, Tech companies k ETF ho sakte hain, nuclear companies k ETF ho sakte hain ya fir, semiconductor ETF's ya future technologies k ETF ko dal sakte hain.
- Large cap, Bluechip companies ko le sakta hu, log run me wo v bahut fayde denge  and will be safe also.
- Debt fund me laga sakta hu.
- Bond v kharid sakte hain. (the most safest form of investement)
- Real estate k business v ho sakte hain.
- Physical gold v ho sakte hain.

### Wealth Grow :-
 
 Iska matlab hai ki wealth grow hoye, bhale hi thora risk lene pade to v sahi hai but purpose is ki grow hoye ye.

- Mid-cap mutual fund:- isme future pottential acha rehta hai.
- Small cap equity:-  jisme ki pottential bahut acha hoye as per my research and bade bade investors uss stock ko kharide ho.
- Short term swing trading kind of thing v kar sakta hu. but mujhe jyada return nai kamana hai, mai ek do percent kam kamaunga but safe khelunga. ye jyaa better hai. 
- International investing:- Isme hum futuristic technology ya high growth rate wali companies ko kharid sakte hain, indexes already humne discuss kar liye ki safe v rhega.. upar me.
