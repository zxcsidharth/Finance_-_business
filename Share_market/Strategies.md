### 1. UP 20âœ… :-
According to this strategy, hum jo upar grouping banaye hain unhi me se companies ko pick karenge like S50, S50_nxt and S250 me. But ye opportunities jyada S50_nxt and S250 me millte hain, kyunki S50 me sari badi companies hoti hain waha pr utna nai mill pata hai. 

Isme hota kya hai na ki jab koi share ka cnadle lagatar 2-3 din tak green hi hai aur around 20% ka jump aaya to iska matlab hai ki Operator(DII, FII, HNI) log virajmaan ho gaye hain. ab fir wo dhire dhie apna paisa nikalenge wapas.
to jab wo nikal lete hain to usske bad uss stock me bss bach jate hain retail investor and uss samay wo acha buying point hota hai. 

**CASE 1**:- 
- hum candle stick graph me jaa kar ye dekhte hain ki aisa kab hua hai ki maximum 3 green candles mill kar atleast 20% ka growth diye ho kahin pr, ya 2 candle mill kar diye ho ya even ek single v diya hai to chalege. 
- So ek baar ye pata chal gaya ki iss jagah pr 20% ka growth milla hai to uss case me
- waha pr horizontal line laga do uppar aur lower me. 
- uske baad hmko wait karna hai ki wo lower line ko kab wapas touch karege..aur jab touch kar jayega to uske baad hmko buy karna hai. but agar buy point k aash pass agar support dikhe aur yadi wo purane support k upar hai to wo aur jyada positive sign hai buy karne k liye.  aur pichle support se niche jaa rhe hain to risky hai usme paisa lagana
- and then wait karna hai ki ye wapas kab uss top line ko touch karega, jaha pr se candle bada green hua tha, aur jab touch karege to hmko bechna hai turant fir uske bad.
- Chuki ye movement Operator log karte hain to uss case me jab graph wapas uss candle pr aa jata hai to iska matlab ki operator ne apna profit book kar liya hai, aur ab hum usme invest kar sakte hain. 
- Agar sab sahi shatth raha to 20 % ka profit book ho jayega.
- Ye strategy se hum operator(jo stock me heavy paisa dalte hain) ka behaviour predict kar pate hain.. because wo paisa dalenge to badhega and then hum v usi ko follow karenge.

NOTE:- yad rhe ki 3 candle se jyada ka nai dekhna hai.

***Why this strategy works?***
because bade bade investors ya companies aksar market se paisa ugane k liye kya karti hain ki 
paisa laga kar stock ko upar le jati hain..to uss samay pr agar lagayenge to nuksan hone k chances hain. so better ye hota hai ki jab wo apne lower ko touch kar jaye tab kharide..kyunki wo investors apna profit book kar chuke hote hain aur fir book kar k kuch time baad fir koi investors aayenge dobara. tab uss samay hamara profit badhega.

Also hamara paisa dubne ka chance kam hai because humne bahut badhiya company me paisa dala hai to in long run waise v wo stock badhega hi.. bss time ki baat hai ki kab badhega.

Also jab next time investors paisa dalenge usme to uss time wo pichli barr k mukable 1% jyada hi return le jayenge because kuch time me inflation v add ho jata hai. 
#### <u>Exception</u>
Kabhi kabhi aisa v hota hai ki hamne buy kara aur uske baad share girta hi chala gaya..to uss case me hmko ek aur kaam karna hoga ki aur past data dekhna hoga ki past me v kab 3/2/1 candles mill kar 20% growth diye the. aur fir jaha v millega to waha pr v horizontal line draw karo. and then fir jab girte girte uss lowest line ko v touch kar le to then buy more and then sell the same amount of share once it pass the top line of the margin, and then wait for price to go up further to sell jo humne pehli bar kharide the.

Or else kai bar wo buy point k niche chala gaya to wait karna hai, kyunki wo fir upar jayega, aur agr uss point se 20 % tak niche chala jaata hai to uss case me aur kuch share kharid lene hain taki loss ka average ho jaye.

Isme jaruri nai ki wo 20% Up ho tabhi uthayenge, wo 15-20% ktak ka hum dekh sakte hain.
#### <u>Funds Allocation</u>
a good practice is jitna hum ek sal me invest karne ka soche hain usme ka 9% hi ek stock me invest kare. and out of that 9% divide it into 3 parts with 3% each. and ek bar me 3 % hi invest karen..uske bad agar wo 3% gadbad ho jaye to aur 3% buy kare..so that loss ko kam kiya jaa sake.

#### <u>Game of support and resistance</u>
Resistance upar wala point hota hai jaha pr ki graph touch kar raha hai, aur support niche wala point hota hai jaha pr ki graph touch kar raha hai. to kai baar aisa hota hai ki jo resistance hai wo new support ban jata hai jab resistance ko break kar jata hai to wo ek naya support banayega jo ki ek time pr resistance hota tha, aur usi tarah se ab ek new resistance ban jayega.

But isme agar aisa hota hai ki purana support jo tha usse niche me aakar new support banta hai to uss case me hame uss stock me invest nai karna chahiye because ek support ko tod kar ek new support banayega aur wo v purane wale k niche me to wo downtrend ka signal hota hai. aur hame waha invest nai karna hai.
#### <u>SMA in UP 20</u>
Hum isme v SMA laga kar dekh sakte hain but yaha pr agar SMA ka jo tarika hai waisa na reh kar aisa v raha to consider kar sakte hain like:- jo price hai wo black line k kareeb hoye aur bhale hi black line niche ho uske upar red, and use upar green. to agar price red green se niche aa kar agar black k pass khada ho gaya hai to uss case me hum isko consider kar sakte hain.

ye strategy short me profit earn karne ka tarike hai. matlab intraday wale waise to jyada use karta hu, but Hum isko 10 days tak me profit banane k liye use karenge. isme 97% chance hota hai uptrend jane ka. aur fir agar RSI laga de ya Piotroski use karenge to half % chance aur badh jayega upar jane ka.

## 5. BTST or BTSD Strategy

- this is applicable to all group of companies not just confined to S50, S_50_nxt and S200 etc.
- iss strategy me hota hai ki aaj kharidna hai kal bechna hai.
- Buy karna hai between 3:15 to 3:30
- Sell karna hai agle din jab market open hota hai 9:15 to 9:30 tak
- Isko usi din karna hai jis din hum ghar pr hoye khali hain. Job k period me nai karna hai.
- Ye market volatility pr depende karta hai.
- Isme 15 min ka chart bss dekhna hai.
- Isme minimum 10k lagana hi hota hai.
- Isme share ko hmko over night hold karna hai. 
- Isme stop loss jarur lagana hai 2-3% ka hai.
- Aise stock me trade karna hai, jo BSE and NSE me v listed hain because usse hmko liquidity jyada milta hai.
- Isme hmko volume chart v dekhna hai.
- NSE ki website ko track karna hai.
#### <u>When to buy and sell?</u>
- 2-3 bje k bich me green candle jyada hoye. it means ki buying hona chahiye na ki selling.
- Fir volume chart dekhenge aur fir dekho ki volume uss time pr high hona chahiye. especially 3:15 k baad me
- 3rd condition ye hai ki price jo hai wo SMA ka black line aur green line se niche aana chahiye. 
- but yad rhe ki hmko ye sab chij 3:15 se 3:30 k bich me hi dekhna hai.
#### <u>How much profit to take?</u>
- Agar hmko 5% ka profit mill gaya 9:30 se pehle hi to exit kar lena hai.
- ya fir 9:30 tak to exit kar hi lena hai, bhale 5% ka profit na v ho to.
- It means ki dono me se jo pehle aa jaye usko kar lena hai.
- aur agar 2-3% ka stop loss hit kar jaye usi bich me to fir to baat hi khatam hai.
#### <u>Why this strategy works?</u>
- kyunki intraday wale 2 bje k baad close hone tak apna sell off kar dete hain. to uss case me stock ka price stable and niche aa jata hai.
- Fir agle din market khulte hi ja kar sell karte hain kyunki intraday wale apna position le lete hain market khulte hi, to unme buy ka pressure hota hai. to iss wajah se thora sa daam uchal jata hai.
- Isme SMA ka bahut role hai. leki bss 2 SMA lagega isme.
	- ek lagega 25 ka aur dusra wala 50 ka. But do hi kyun?
	- Iska karan hai ki ek hum 15 min ka chart dekh rhe hain, aur ek din me from 9:15 to 3:30 tak 25 time 15 min ka slot hota hai. aur do din me yehi 50 min ka slot hota hai. to iss liye hum yaha pr 25 aur 50 ka lagayenge ki ek din kharidne wala aur ek dusre din bechne wala lagayenge.

#### Working of Rob booker indicator:-
- ye indicator counter trend ko batata hai means ki suppose share gir raha hai agar, to ye batayega ki ab uth sakta hai. similarly agar share uthta jaa raha hai to ye batata hai ki kab se girne ka chance jyada hoga.. ye batayega ki yaha chance jyada hai to ab bech kar nikal lo.
- Ye indicator hmko S50 ya S50_next me hi lagana hai to badhiya kaam dega.
- **Ab sawal ye hai ki kaise pata chalega ki kab kharidna hai?**
	- to wo aise ki jab v aisa line bane jisme ki left wala point upar ho aur right wala chorr niche ho to iska matlab ye bolta hai ki lene ka chance ban raha hai( yani ki iske uthne ka chance ban raha hai ab). aur agar iske ulta batata hai to matlab ki iske girne ka chance bann raha hai. (yani isko blte hain ki uptrend khatam ho raha hai)
	- Ab apne jankari ko aur pukhta karne k liye mai volume ka data dekh kar analyse kar sakta hu ki kisme kya bann raha hai.
	- Ye buying k liye sabse jyada badhiya rehta hai

#### How does RSI work?
- RSI ranges from 30 - 70.
- RSI me agar kooi stock ka 70 k upar dikha raha hai to matlab hai ki wo Overbought ho chuka hai.
- agar 30 k under hai to iska matlab hai ki Under bought hai abhi ya oversold keh sakte hain. 
- Aur agar bich me hai kahin pr like 30 -70 k bich me to iska matlab hai ki wo abhi flat hai..
- To usi tarah se agar hmko sirf RSI k dam pr kharidna hai to aise kharido
  - Agar 30 k niche aa jaye RSI aur fir bounce back karte time 30 k range me aa jaye to uss time pr kharid lena hai but yad rhe ki kharidte time ye BB ka jo median line hai usko nai paar karna chahiye. agar usko parr kar jata hai to samjho ki gadbad ho jayega.
