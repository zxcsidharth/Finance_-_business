### How to find the S250 companies list?
iss list ko hum banate hain for short term profit. so that BB startegy ya dusra kuch strategy perform kiya jaa sake. So the point that we should consider for this list is the following:- 

- ROCE & ROE > 12
- Debt to equity < 0.5
- Pledge percentage = 0
- YoY quaterly profit growth > 0
- YoY quaterly sale growth > 0
- Net profit > 150 (agar list me kafi kam aayengi to uss case me 80 ya 100 crore v rakh sakte hain.)
- Promoter holding + FII + DII > public holding. (isko hata v sakte hain filter me se aur excel me ye kaam kar sakte hain)
- Piotroski score > 5

- Then sare companies k chart pattern dekho, jiska chart pattern acha nai hai to usko hata denge list se.
- aur ye v dekh lo ki kahin public holding kisi me bahut jyada to nai hai.
- Ye upar wale indicator banks k liye nai hain. 

banks k case me ROCE & ROE hum greater than 5% dekhte hain and DII, FII jarura dekhna hai > public holding and also Debt to equity ko dekhna hi nai hai banks and NBFC's k liye
