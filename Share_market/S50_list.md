
### How to make S50, S50 nxt ?

Jab v kisi sector se companies ko list down kara to usme se filter out karne k liye pehle ye chijen dekhe. 

1. Unhi companies ko dekhna hai jjo NSE & BSE me listed ho, sirf BSE me hone se nai hoga.
2. P/E ratio agar bahut high hai to nikal dena hai uss company ko.
3. Debt to Equity ratio should not be more than 0.15 (15%) But banks and NBFC's k liye ye nai dekhna hai..( ye kitna rakhna hai iske bare me thora aur research karna hai)
4. Profit growth for the 3 years agar negative hai to hatao.
5. ROCE agar 13% se niche hai to hatao.
6. Average ROE for 3 year should not be negative, 
7. OPM agar negative me hai to hata do usko aankh band kar k.
8. agar kisi company ka public holding agar jyada hai to wo acha baat nai hai. promoter holding ka jyada hona acha baat hai. so promoter holding + DII's + FII's agar acha hai aur public holding bahut kam hai to wo badhiya company hota hai.
9. Check the profit growth in three year and agar wo bahut kam hai to remove them.
10. Fir jo bacha hua company hai usme dekhe ki baki k data jo available hain usme kya sab kuch thik hain?
11. Agar kisi company ka data available nai hai to unn companies ko remove karna hai jinka data hi available nai hai kuch kuch chij ka. 
12. kai baar kuch companies me sab thik dikhega but DII &FII bahut kam hai ya zero hai. to unn companies ko v hata dena hai. kyunki agar public k pass bahut share hain to wo acha baat nai hain, because shares ka growth ko DII's and FII's hi direct karte hain. to wo agar hain hi nai to company k growth ko public aur promoters hi chala rhe hain.. and public ka holdings pr hum paisa nai lafga sakte kyunki aadhe se jyada log kisi k kehne me le rakhe hote hain. To wo kal ko ek chota news aane pr apne paise nikal k bhag v sakte hain.
   So ek general sa formula hai ki FII's + DII's holdings agar public holding se jkyada hai to put them in the list.
   
   Sometimes aisa v hota hai ki promotrs holding kafi kam hai ya zero hai aur DII's and FII's agar badhiya hai aur public holding kam hai to uss case me v consider kar sakte hain waisi companies ko.
10. Then jo bacha hua companies ka list hai unme se Market cap k aadhar pr usko sort kar lena hai aur then jo sabse kam cap ki company hai aur jo sabse bada cap ka company hai usme ye dekhna hai ki jo sabse kam cap ki companies hain wo ya sabse bade cap ki company k 5- 10% k andar v hai kya? agar nai hai to remove that. 
11. Fir jo bacha hua list hain unme se ye dekho ki kya baki sara aankde badhiya hain, but kai companies k kuch thik hoga to kuch aur gadbad hoga to uss case me hame thora aur analyse karna padega. like:-
    - agar debt jyada hai to kya baki chijen thik hain? 
    - Agar PE jyada hai to kya baki sare chijen thik hain? PE kahin extremely jyada to nai hai na? like ROCE.
    - Kuch company hoga jo DEBT v hoga aur ROCE v koi bahut badhiya nai hoga to hmko badhiya wale k pass jana hai.
      
12. Pledging v jarur check akrna hai har comnpany ko final list me dalne se pehle. agar bahut kam pledging hua hai to ek bar screener me detail me dekho ki pleadging hua hai to kyun hua hai aur fir usko unpledge kara hai ya nai company walon ne. agar unpledge hua hai ya pledging kar k apne r&D pr paisa lagaya gaya hai to ek bar ko company k baki k indicators ko dekhte hue consider kar sakte hain.
13. Fir jo list bach gaya companies k unn sabke 3-5 sal k graph dekhne hain, agar graph poor hai to uss case me unn companies ko hmko remove karna hai. matlab ki 3 sal me graph dekh kar return dikhna chahiye.
	- But here is a contradiction that, agar company 3 sal me return nai di hai to uska ROCE and ROE acha kaise ho sakta hai, because ROCE agar acha hai tabhi to iss list me exist karti hai?. so try to get the answer for this.

BUT AGAR HUM CHAHTE HAIN KI EK HI BAAR ME S50 AND S50_NXT NIKAL DE TO USS CASE ME HAME YE KARNA HOGA.
- screener me bss ye filter lagao ki market cap > 2000 cr uske baad sabko download kar lo aur kuch basic operation karne k bad like (roce, debt to equity, profit growth, opm, 3 year avaerage retrun, and some other info ) k baad industry wale column ko filter karna hoga and then jo v companies hongi same industries me unko peers comparison karna hoga. 
- fir same peers wali companies ka graph dekh kar filter out karenge ki kaun kaun ka graph thik nai hai. fir jo bachega uss industry ka uspr below step apply karenge. 
- and then same peers companies ko karne k baad jo jo badhiya ho aur jiska graph badhiya ho to fir usme baki k kuch kuch acha point dekh lena hai jaise ki net profit badh raha hai ki nai. and cash flow badh raha hi ki nai, DII, FII and promoters holding.. basically balance sheet and P&L analysis. 
- Fir uske baad bachega jo usme se hum compare karenge P/E ratio vs Industry p/e ratio, jo v company ka p/e ratio agar industry p/e k double hai to usko hata dena hai. 
- at last list agar bada hai to ROE < 13 % hai to unko v hatao.
- Fir final list aane k baad har sector k companies ka market cap k aadhar pr usko S50 ya S50_nxt ye jayega, like agar market cap higher hai to usko S50 me dalenge aur nai to S50_nxt ye bhejenge. but remember that wo companies jo uss sector ki badi company hai uske 7% v  nai hai to usko hata dena hai list se. 

***Q. Why S50 and S50_nxt ?***
-> Because S50 gives the stability and S50_nxt gives the growth in long term and short term.
