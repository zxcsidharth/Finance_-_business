Bazar se paisa uthane k liye companies ko do bhag me divide kar diya gaya hai.
1. **Main-board companies**:- Ye wo companies hain jo 100 cr se jyada paisa uthana chahti hain market se. Ye NSE aur BSE dono me list down hote hain.
2. **SME**:- ye wo companies hain jo 100 cr se kam paisa uthana chah rhi hain. inko NSe ya BSe me se kisi ek me hi jaa sakte hain.
