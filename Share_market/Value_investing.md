- 7:- ye hai PE ratio, chuki hum man chuke hain ki ye 0 growth company hai to PE ratio iska 7 mante hain, Halaki agar company thora thik thak raha to uss case me iss 7 k jagah hum 8.5 man lenge, Jaise ki niche hero motocorp k case me humne mana hai.
- 1G:- Yani ki one times growth rate of EPS.
